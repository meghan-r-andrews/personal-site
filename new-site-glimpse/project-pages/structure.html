This was made for a web-design class at Virginia Commonwealth University, where I learned HTML, CSS and a little bit of Javascript.

Our assignment was to create a website that referenced a structure in some way. I chose to reference a piano/keyboard. I wanted the viewer to be able to "play the piano" by clicking the keys. But instead of "piano sounds" coming out of each key, a song would play instead.

I wanted to make my website perceivable to viewers who are deaf or hard of hearing, which is why I included the lyrics and sound descriptions. I decided to approach the sound descriptions as if I were describing the songs to a friend.
